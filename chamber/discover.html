Fortaleza's Economy

Fortaleza's economy thrives on tourism, commerce, and industry. Key sectors include textile production, food processing, and technology. Its strategic coastal location also supports a growing port economy, boosting exports and imports, while tourism drives hospitality and services, attracting millions of visitors annually.

Fortaleza-CE

Fortaleza, the capital of Ceará, is known for its beautiful beaches, warm climate, rich Northeastern culture, and vibrant nightlife. Popular destinations include Praia do Futuro and Beach Park.

Fortaleza Hub Commerce

The Hub Commerce is a business chamber focused on fostering collaboration and unity among companies in Fortaleza. It offers networking opportunities, business resources, and advocates for the interests of its members.
