NP Membership Benefits

The Non-Profit (NP) Membership is tailored for organizations dedicated to community service and non-profit efforts. Joining at this level provides you with:

  • Feature your organization in our directory to reach a broader audience and potential supporters
  • Connect with like-minded organizations and industry leaders through exclusive events and webinars
  • Receive tools, templates, and educational materials to support your mission and operations.
  • Benefit from special rates on consulting, marketing, and software solutions tailored for non-profits

Make a difference while connecting with businesses that share your values!

Bronze Membership Benefits

The Bronze Membership provides small businesses and startups with opportunities for growth and increased visibility. This membership offers:

  • Showcase your company in our directory to attract potential customers and partners.
  • Gain access to promotional tools and resources to boost your brand visibility.
  • Participate in member-exclusive events to connect with other businesses and industry experts.
  • Enjoy reduced rates on ads across our platform to enhance your outreach.

Perfect for businesses just getting started and looking to make a mark.

Silver Membership Benefits

The Silver Membership is designed for established businesses seeking advanced support and services. This membership provides:

  • Receive dedicated assistance from our support team to address your business needs promptly.
  • Access sophisticated marketing resources and analytics to enhance your outreach efforts.
  • Engage in partnerships and collaborations with other members to expand your network.
  • Attend members-only workshops and training sessions to develop skills and industry knowledge.

Ideal for businesses looking to expand their reach and strengthen their local presence.

Gold Membership Benefits

The Gold Membership is our top-tier option, crafted for industry leaders and large organizations. This membership provides:

  • Attend VIP networking events and conferences featuring industry experts and thought leaders.
  • Enjoy personalized assistance and consulting tailored to your organization's specific needs.
  • Increase your visibility through featured placements and sponsorship options across our platform.
  • Receive comprehensive industry reports and data analysis to inform your strategic decisions.

This membership tier is ideal for businesses aiming to assume a leadership position in the local commerce community.
