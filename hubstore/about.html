About Us – Welcome to HubStore!

At HubStore, we believe that technology shapes the future, and we’re here to make sure everyone has access to the latest innovations at the best prices. From our humble beginnings to becoming your go-to online marketplace for all things electronic, this is our story.

Our Journey: From Vision to Reality

  • Founded in 2020: It all started with a dream – to create a central hub for electronics lovers. We wanted to build a marketplace that not only sold devices but connected people with the latest trends and innovations.
  • 2021 - First Big Break: By the end of our first year, we had partnered with over 200 brands, ranging from the most trusted names in tech to niche startups bringing fresh ideas to life.
  • 2022 - Expansion Beyond Boundaries: With demand growing, we expanded our catalog to include smart home gadgets, drones, electric scooters, and virtual reality gear, catering to tech enthusiasts, gamers, and professionals alike.
  • 2024 - Your All-in-One Hub: Today, HubStore offers over 50,000 products across multiple categories, making it easier than ever for our customers to find exactly what they need – whether it’s a powerful gaming laptop, a sleek smartphone, or the latest noise-canceling earbuds.

Our Mission: Technology at Your Fingertips
